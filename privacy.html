Privacy Policy

1. What we collect

We collect product details, order history, and expense records that you manually input into the application.

2. How we use it

Your data is strictly used to power the Dashboard analytics, generate tables, and provide you with business tracking functionalities.

3. Cookie choices

We only use 'Local Storage' to save your language preference and temporary session data. No tracking cookies are used.

4. Third parties

Your data is stored securely via Google Firebase. We do not sell or share your data with any advertising agencies.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us via the Help page.
